FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about working with AETHER. Don't see your question? Reach out directly.

Ask a Question
What types of spaces can you transform?

We've transformed everything from intimate retail boutiques to massive concert halls, museum galleries, hotel lobbies, automotive showrooms, and outdoor architectural facades. Our team assesses each space's unique characteristics to design the optimal immersive experience.

What is the typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A focused installation typically takes 8-12 weeks from concept to deployment. Larger, more complex experiences may require 4-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our discovery phase.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Absolutely. We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including 24/7 remote monitoring, on-site technical support, content updates, and seasonal refreshes. Our goal is ensuring your installation performs flawlessly for years.

What is your pricing structure?

Each project is uniquely priced based on scope, technology requirements, and timeline. We provide detailed proposals after the discovery phase. Projects typically start from $150,000 for focused installations.

Can you work with international clients?

Yes, we have delivered projects across 12 countries and maintain a global network of installation partners. Our team is experienced in managing logistics, compliance, and cultural considerations for international deployments.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We specialize in projection mapping, LED and OLED installations, holographic displays, AR/VR/MR experiences, generative and AI-driven art, motion tracking, spatial audio, and interactive sensor systems. We also develop custom hardware and software when needed.
